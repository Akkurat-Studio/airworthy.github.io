Sign Up for Private Pilot Ground School!

  • Begins March 8th, 2016
  • Fifteen weeks of class every Tuesdays from 6-9pm at Campbell Airport
  • This is a fast paced course covering all you need to know to not only pass your Private Pilot Airplane Knowledge Test but also pass it with a high score. Classes are held at Campbell Airport in the aviation-training classroom.
  • Class size is limited to assure that each participant’s needs are totally met.
  • Tuition is only $385.00 payable in advance to reserve your seat.
  • Success is guaranteed. Over 97% of those completing this course over the past sixteen years have passed the Knowledge Test on their first try.
  • This class is also attended by licensed Private Pilots who use it as part of their continuing education to update and maintain their aviation knowledge.
  • For more information, or to reserve your seat in the class, email us at school@airworthy.co or call Campbell Airport Flight School (847) 223-2200.
